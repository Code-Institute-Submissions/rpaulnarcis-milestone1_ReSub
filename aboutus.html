Our Mission

The fitness industry seems to focus on people who are already fit, which leaves 80% of the population feeling left out. We want to change that. We offer the ultimate mind and body experience; our instructors have a wealth of knowledge in their various fields. Our main focus is women - mainly mothers. We add a gentle touch to our training exercises, yet yield explosive, powerful results where strength and endurance are concerned.  

Exercise Programs

Cardio Drills:

Hi-lo basic cardio drills for all fitness levels to warm up muscles, and generally get you prepared for the workout ahead.

Burn and Build:

Build muscle and burn fat with this high energy full body workout focusing on cardio, strength abs, and core!

Zumba:

Latin-inspired, cardio-driven dance fitness that will leave you feeling alive.

Pump:

Our signature High energy barbell resistance training workout formatted to music.

Strong by Zumba:

Music-led, high-intensity intervals that combine bodyweight, muscle conditioning, cardio, and plyometric training.

Rev N Recover:

Rev up your workout with high intensity, challenging circuits, while recovering with active interval rests for a new complete body workout.

Functional Fitness:

Train your muscles to work together, and prepare them for daily tasks, by simulating common movements you might do at home, at work, or in sports.

Body Sculpting:

A non-aerobic, muscle-toning class, usually focused on core strength. We use a wide range of weight bars, exercise bands, and dumbbells. You perform traditionally weight-training moves in a class setting.
